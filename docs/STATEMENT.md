# Clay Millennium Problem: Yang-Mills Existence and Mass Gap

Jaffe-Witten (2000): Prove that for any compact simple gauge group G,
a non-trivial quantum Yang-Mills theory exists on R^4 with a mass gap
delta > 0, as a well-defined mathematical object satisfying the
Wightman or Osterwalder-Seiler axioms.

Spectral form: construct a measure on SU(3)-connections satisfying
OS0-OS4 with transfer matrix T = exp(-H_YM) such that

    delta = inf spec(H_YM) \ {0} > 0

The KP chain here certifies w1(beta_0) < 1/7 as a conditional
input to OS4. The continuum limit and full OS0-OS4 are not constructed.
